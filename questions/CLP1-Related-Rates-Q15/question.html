Find the rate of change $\frac{dA}{dt}$ of the area of the annulus $\left\{ (x, y) : r^2 \leq x^2 + y^2 \leq R^2 \right\}$. (i.e. the points inside the circle of radius $R$ but outside the circle of radius $r$) if $R= {{params.R}} \; \textrm{cm}$, $r= {{params.r}} \; \textrm{cm}$, $\frac{dR}{dt}= {{params.DR}} \; \frac{\textrm{cm}}{\textrm{s}}$, and $\frac{dr}{dt}= {{params.Dr}} \; \frac{\textrm{cm}}{\textrm{s}}$.
