# Tutorial Derivatives 1 

Find the second derivative.

## sympy.sin(a*x**2)

$$f'(x) = 2*a*x*cos(a*x^{2}) $$

$$f''(x) = 2*a*cos(a*x^{2})-4*a^{2}*x^{2}*sin(a*x^{2})$$

## sympy.cos(b*x)

$$f'(x) = -sin(b*x)*b$$

$$f''(x) = -b^{2}*cos(b*x)$$

## (c/(x+1)**5)

$$f'(x) = \frac{-5*c}{(x+1)^{6}} $$

$$f''(x) = \frac{30*c}{(x+1)^{7}} $$
