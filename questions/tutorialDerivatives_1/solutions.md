# Tutorial Derivatives 1 

Find the second derivative.

## sympy.sin(a*x**2)

$$f'(x) = 2ax\cos(ax^{2}) $$

$$f''(x) = 2a\cos(ax^{2})-4a^{2}x^{2}\sin(ax^{2})$$

## sympy.cos(b*x)

$$f'(x) = -\sin(bx)b$$

$$f''(x) = -b^{2}\cos(bx)$$

## (c/(x+1)**5)

$$f'(x) = \frac{-5c}{(x+1)^{6}} $$

$$f''(x) = \frac{30c}{(x+1)^{7}} $$
