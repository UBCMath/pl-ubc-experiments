# CLP1 2.4 Q5

Find the first derivative.

## Question 1

$$f(x) = a_1 x^{a_2} + a_3 x^{a_4}$$

$$f'(x) = a_1 a_2 x^{a_2 - 1} + a_3 a_4 x^{a_4 - 1}$$

## Question 2

$$f(x) = b_1 x^{b_2} + \frac{b_3}{b_4} x^{\frac{b_5}{b_6}}$$

$$f'(x) = b_1 b_2 x^{b_2 - 1} + \frac{b_3}{b_4} \frac{b_5}{b_6} x^{\frac{b_5}{b_6} - 1}$$
